When you have an idea, complex concept or emotion that you want to pass on to someone else, you use some or all of the different types of media that human beings have developed and evolved through-out our history on this planet. The art of effective communication is one of our greatest and most valuable gifts. Different media can now be combined to maximize the effectiveness of communication. This is what is meant by the term 'Multimedia'. It is the combination of language, sound and visual images using Video, CD, DVD and of course, the Internet. When you include the added dimension of Interactivity, the picture is complete. Now the audience can participate in the communication experience. Interaction gives the user control over the information they are accessing and greatly enhances their experience in education, training, sales, and entertainment.

We are a company born and raised in the heartland of Australia. We started out in 2007 with humble beginnings at an internet café trying to help out a mother and daughter put their face on the internet. Soon after, we couldn’t keep up with the demand and formed a company called Taylored Media. From there, we have built our steps day by day adding 2 more members to the staff of the Taylored Media family. Now there are 4 of us coding, imaging and communicating along the coast of Australia, fine-tuning great business’ and their websites. Take a step into Multimedia with us and you will get to know the Taylored Media’s talent and vision for your company’s website.

At Taylored Media we believe that the best results are achieved by using the best people in the production of each of the different media we therefore use a range of specialist providers to produce different media. For example video is a specialist field that requires the best in equipment and people and we thus use the services of a specialist video production studio to produce these elements of our media, the same goes for all the other elements. Since 2000, Taylored Media has specialized in the production of internet communication solutions. We tailor make your web site to your requirements, in accordance with sound design principals.
